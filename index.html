To flash your ESP32 you will likely need to hole down the right (IO0) button before clicking on "Install"

To flash your GDO:

  1. Hold down the “Reset” (left) button on the GDO
  2. Click “Install”
  3. Hold down the “BOOT” button while still holding the “Reset” button
  4. Release the “Reset” button
  5. Release the “BOOT” button
